Hi there! I'm Ian.

I'm a composer, conductor, theatre-maker, linguist, and multioriented researcher whose interests are at the junction between effective communication and equitable representation. I'm currently on a leave of absence from Harvard College, as I pursue a few fun projects:

  • Music Directing and Orchestrating for the American Repertory Theater 's Family Show, Jack and the Beanstalk: A Musical Adventure;
  • Serving as Events Intern with the Center for Asian American Media;
  • Completing a Graduate-Level Professional Certificate in Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing from Stanford University; and
  • Performing original research with the Schlesinger Library at the Radcliffe Institute of Advanced Study on the poetics of Asian/American Migratory Legacy.

Thanks for coming to my website! If you're interested in learning more about me or my work, feel free to check out any of the tabs above or the social media links below. Don't hesitate to get in touch with me; I'm always looking to meet fellow artists, scholars, and collaborators! Drop an email at ianchan [at] college.harvard.edu.
